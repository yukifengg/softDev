Paragraphs have a whole new design! They're formatted to make text look nicers while being robust when you change screen size.

For example: class="callout large primary text-center"

What you see above you are two cool functions of Foundation. Using the grid class, you can make grids (duh!). Inside the grid are two cells, which are cards. We like grids because it helps make laying out your info easy (YAY!) We like cards because adding a picture with a caption is pretty common for websites, and cards make things look dope!
